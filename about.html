AS A SINGER
The name IU is a combination of 'I' and 'you' which means "you and I get along through music. Her fandom Uaena was founded on 17/7/2017 after 9 years of her debut.
She became a trainee under Kakao M (formerly LOEN Entertainment, now Kakao Entertainment ) in 2007 and debuted as a singer at the age of 15 with the album Lost and Found.
IU then released two albums, growing up and iu...im, respectively, which were both successful in the domestic market. The lead single "Good day" from the album Real released in 2010 topped Korea's Gaon digital music chart for 5 weeks.
With the success of her 2011 albums, Real+ and Last Fantasy, IU has established herself as a formidable force in the domestic market. That year's album helped her win many awards and become an A-list star. Since then, she has also been favored by the public to give her the nickname the nation's little sister. Since then, iu became famous with the public and her career quickly rose to the ranks of A-list stars. She was also the most successful solo singer at that time. She began to gain more public attention and actively produced music that achieved great success.
